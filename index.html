Student Registation

New to our platform? Registering as a student is a straightforward process. Simply navigate to the registration page and provide the required information, including your full name, email address, department, course, and other necessary details. After a successful registration, you'll have your own account, granting you access to a wealth of educational resources and features tailored to enhance your learning journey.

Student Login And Profile

Existing students can securely access their personalized accounts by entering their credentials. Once logged in, students gain access to a range of features, including viewing their course details, checking grades, and interacting with course materials. Our platform ensures a seamless and user-friendly experience, making it easy for students to stay connected with their academic progress.

Admin Login And Dashboard

Admins play a crucial role in managing and overseeing the system. Admin login provides access to a dashboard for viewing all the students in the system. From here, administrators can efficiently handle tasks such as adding or removing users, monitoring system activity, and ensuring the smooth operation of the platform. Security measures are in place to safeguard admin access, ensuring the integrity and confidentiality of the system's data.
